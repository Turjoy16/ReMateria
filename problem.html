The Plastic Problem

Plastic pollution is one of the most pressing environmental issues of our time. Every year, millions of tons of plastic enter our oceans, harming marine life and ecosystems. On land, plastic waste clogs landfills, contaminates soil, and releases harmful chemicals.

Microplastics, tiny particles of plastic, have been found in everything from our drinking water to our food. The long-term effects of microplastic exposure on human health are still unknown, but they are a growing concern for scientists and public health officials.
